##### Divine Existence vs. Divine Attributes

For example, in Tawhid (Monotheism), what is the limit of the intellect? In Tawhid, the limit of the intellect is only that it can prove the existence of Allah. That Allah exists, there is a creator, a creator who has created us. But can the intellect now tell the details of this creator? The intellect can never tell the details of this creator. That is, a person can understand the details of that creator only as much as that creator introduces himself. A person cannot understand more than that.
